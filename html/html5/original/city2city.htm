city2City

city2City is a real time shortest path suggesion tool to make your journey faster with complete pre-estimation of time and the journey route

: How to use :

  1. Provide City1 & City 2 Name from anywhere in the world. [ex. Mumbai & Pune]
  2. Now Click on the 'Get Directions' Button.
  3. You will then be able to see graphical route right on the screen map.
  4. Now For detailed path guidance, click on the 'Show Me Detailed Path' link.
  5. Now, A small window with detailed path break-up segments, would be visible on your screen, To get graphical details for each path segment, just click on that particular segment description.
