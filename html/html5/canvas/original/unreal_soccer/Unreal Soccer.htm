Unreal Soccer is a fast-paced strategic action game of association football with simple rules. The teams are not quite what you'd expect; there're pirates, ninjas, robots, space aliens, and more. As you beat higher-rated teams, they become available for you to use against even tougher opponents. This game is implemented using web technology only, i.e. no Flash or Java. You need a good web browser such as Firefox or Opera 9 to play.
